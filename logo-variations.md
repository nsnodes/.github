## Recommendations

**For GitHub README:** Option 1 (current), 2, or 7 - classic ASCII art that renders well

**For Terminal/CLI branding:** Option 8 `[ n s n o d e s ]` - minimal and clean

**For conceptual/thematic:** Option 4 or 9 - emphasizes the "nodes" network concept

**For modern/bold:** Option 2 (Block Style) - high visibility, professional look
